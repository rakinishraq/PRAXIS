A beloved and trusted friend of the Air Temples who contributes a ton philosophically, provides manual labor, is a master healer and emotionally consoles those who ever need it.

"Chakan" ends up being a false identity for "Arthit".

Uses the same bending forms and concepts behind the fire healing, but as a means to burn a potential victim from the inside out.

His blindness has caused him to be highly attuned to the spiritual world given he can’t see the physical world.
